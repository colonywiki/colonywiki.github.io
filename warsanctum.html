Strategic Uses

  • Saint Air Superiority

    The Saint is a self regenerating Air unit that is fast, missile resistant, has long range and deadly air-to-air weaponry. This unit is often desired and rushed for as soon as you can in the early to mid-game where it is most effective.

  • The Sphinx

    The Sphinx, this one unit has the highest health in the game and features Composite Armor, it absorbs powerful attacks like nothing but weak to small arms fire like Infantry. This units anti-air weaponry is superb, and will deny the airspace for the enemy. This one unit can be the ultimate frontline beast.
