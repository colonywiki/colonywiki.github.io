Combat Strategies

  • Anti T3 Specialist

    If your enemy is constructing a lot of black queens, hover tanks or sakatas, build this unit to shred through their expensive armor. The Jötunn's ground weaponry is specifically calibrated to penetrate special armors, making it exceptionally effective against high-tier units.

  • Defensive Anchor Point

    Position Jötunns at the front to create powerful space. Their dual weapon systems allow them to engage both ground and air threats simultaneously, making them perfect for holding space against mixed assault forces.

  • Support Column Integration

    While the Jötunn moves slowly, it excels when integrated into a combined arms approach. Pair with faster units that can screen and protect against infantry threats, allowing the Jötunn to focus its devastating firepower on high-value targets without being overrun by cheaper counter units.

Strengths

  • Against Special Armor

    The Jötunn's ground and air weapons are built to pierce through Special Armor, making it the perfect counter to expensive enemy units. Its high damage output can quickly neutralize threats that would otherwise dominate the battlefield.

  • Ironclad Defense

    The Jötunn features the most powerful composite armor available, specifically designed to withstand the high-damage attacks of most tier 3 units. This survivability ensures it remains operational even under heavy fire, providing sustained value throughout extended engagements.

Weaknesses

  • Infantry Vulnerability

    The armor of the Jötunn is vulnerable to infantry weapons. Units such as the Missile Marine, A25-Romans, S-Marines and Legionnaires will efficiently penetrate its defenses, quickly melting through its otherwise formidable protection.

  • Limited Mobility

    With a top speed of only 0.15m/s, the Jötunn is among the slowest units on the battlefield. This lack of mobility makes it vulnerable to being outmaneuvered and outranged by faster units that can strike from a distance and retreat before the Jötunn can effectively respond.
